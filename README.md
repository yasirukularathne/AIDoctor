# 🏥 AI Doctor: Conversational Medical Assistant 🤖🩺  
An **AI-powered virtual doctor** that engages in interactive **voice and text-based medical consultations**, offering insights and guidance based on symptoms and medical images. 🚀💡  

## 🌟 Key Features  
- 🔊 **Voice-based Symptom Analysis** – Describe your symptoms verbally, and AI will analyze them in real-time.  
- 🖼️ **Medical Image Assessment** – Upload medical images (X-rays, skin conditions, etc.) for AI-powered interpretation.  
- ❓ **Follow-up Questioning** – The AI dynamically asks clarifying questions to refine diagnosis suggestions. (Version2) 
- 💬 **Conversational UI** – Engage in a natural chat-like experience with a sleek, professional interface.  
- 🔉 **Audio Responses** – Hear the AI doctor’s insights using realistic **text-to-speech** technology.  
- 📜 **Consultation History** – Track past interactions and reference previous assessments anytime.  

## 🛠️ Technologies Used  
- 🚀 **Python & Gradio** – For a responsive and interactive web interface.  
- 🧠 **Groq’s LLM API** – Powers medical reasoning and decision-making.  
- 🗣️ **Speech-to-Text & Text-to-Speech** – Enables smooth voice interactions.  
- 🎨 **Modern UI Design** – Aesthetic **dark blue & black theme** with **glassmorphism** elements for a futuristic feel.  

## ⚠️ Disclaimer  
This application is for **educational and demonstration purposes only**. It is **not** a replacement for **professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment**. Always consult a **qualified healthcare provider** for medical concerns.  

✅ **Privacy & Ethics First** – User data is handled securely, ensuring responsible AI usage in healthcare.
